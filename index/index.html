About us

Founded in 1964, our soda brand has always been driven by a passion for innovation and design. Inspired by the Bauhaus aesthetic, we blend the principles of form and function to create beverages that are as visually striking as they are delicious. Each of our sodas is crafted with care, reflecting a perfect balance of simplicity, quality, and bold flavor.

From our humble beginnings to today, we've stayed true to our commitment to excellence. We believe that a great soda should not only refresh but also inspire. That's why we’ve made it our mission to bring you unique, high-quality drinks that stand out on the shelf and leave a lasting impression, following our four main pillars of our company:

Wallet-Friendly

Great taste doesn’t have to break the bank. Our sodas offer premium quality at a price everyone can enjoy.

Organic ingredients

Our sodas are crafted using only the finest organic ingredients, ensuring a natural and refreshing experience with every sip.

Eco-Friendly Packaging

Our packaging is designed with the environment in mind, using materials that are 100% recyclable and eco-friendly.

Quality

Every step of our process is carefully controlled to ensure you get the very best in every bottle.
